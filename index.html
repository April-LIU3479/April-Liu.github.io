Publications

A Novel Underwater Image Enhancement Algorithm and an Improved Underwater Biological Detection Pipeline
Zheng Liu, Yaoming Zhuang*, Pengrun Jia, Chengdong Wu, Hongli Xu, and Zhanlin Liu.
[JMSE 2022]  /  Paper  /  Code

This work presents the first integration of a self-adaptive global histogram stretching (SAGHS) enhancement scheme with an attention-augmented YOLOv5 detector, removing the reliance on hand-crafted priors or large paired datasets common in underwater pipelines and markedly boosting detection robustness. Extensive experiments on the URPC2021 benchmark show that our pose-level accuracy outperforms existing underwater detectors and sets a new state of the art, while simultaneously delivering superior visual fidelity in enhanced views.
